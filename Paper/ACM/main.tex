% The body is typeset for IEEEtran's wider column; acmart's narrower sigconf
% column leaves a few monospace paths / long hyphenated terms slightly over.
% Let TeX stretch as a last resort to soak up most of those residual boxes.
\setlength{\emergencystretch}{3em}
